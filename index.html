Koshish is an independent NGO created by Sahil that sells a line of handmade products from the rural parts of India. The carftes are made using natural materials such as jute and colourful wood, etc, to make various home accessories. His current offerings include trays, coasters, utility baskets, tissue boxes, table mats, and pen holders, among others.

By Arnav Grover and Kevin Jackson of Sancta Maria International School
