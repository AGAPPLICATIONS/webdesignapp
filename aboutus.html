Koshish- About Us

"I don’t need easy. I just need possible.”




Koshish is an NGO that sells handmade products made of materials such as jute and wood . It was created by Sahil, who suffers from Down's Syndrome,leading to various physical and mental impaiments.Overcoming these challenges, he learned how to craft paper bags and envelopes at a vocational center which started an undying flame and passion within him to create these handicrafts. He works 4-6 hours a day and finds his work exciting and fulfilling. He crafts and sells these products independently. He also enjoys painting, listening to music, and dancing.

By Arnav Grover and Kevin Jackson of Sancta Maria International School
